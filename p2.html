Project 2: Emergent Interaction, Exquisite Corpse

Combining two different personalities:

-Needs to represent you as a designer or things that inspire you.

-Can be any color and of anything.

-Canvas is 12 inches by 12 inches split up into 16 squares or 4x4.

-Each person gets the top two squares on the left and the top two on the right. Once each person has finished with their drawing. The drawings are exchanged and then the next two squares below are filled in. At the end, each person will have filled in 8 squares.

-Composed with vector drawings.

-Trying to combine as well as contrast between two different art styles can bring about an amazing mash up. Embracing these differences through an exquisite corpse can accent and strengthen both sides!
