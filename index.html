Project 1: A Visual Message for the Inhabitants of Other Celestial Bodies

Our universe makes up cells, molecules, bodies, and planets of a different somewhat bigger than our universe that we might not even be aware of. These cells, molecules, planets, organs, and bodies can be part of a universe of many other things. Each level is more complex than the one before it but at the same time can get even more complex from going to universe or cell.
